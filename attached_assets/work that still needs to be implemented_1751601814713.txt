### Why you see “it *looks* finished”

*The CSS & DOM shell are immaculate*, so every button, column, and toggle *visually exists*. That makes the page feel “almost done.” But under the hood many handlers are placeholders, and some heavy work still blocks the UI thread. The punch‑list above is everything that still separates the beautiful façade from the fully‑functional site described in your own site map.

Once those items are in place:

* The 3rd branch will *match every functional bullet* of the site map,
* keep the high‑FPS scrolling from the prototype, and
* retain its present pixel‑perfect design.
