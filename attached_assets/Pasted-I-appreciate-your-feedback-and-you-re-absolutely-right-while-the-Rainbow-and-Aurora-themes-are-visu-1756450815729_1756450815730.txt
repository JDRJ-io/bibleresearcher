### Enhanced Rainbow Theme: "Covenant Radiance"

**Inspiration**: A majestic rainbow as God’s covenant sign (Genesis 9:13-16), transformed into a radiant, heavenly display. A video of a rainbow over a golden Jerusalem landscape (symbolizing divine promise) sets the stage, with animated halos and prismatic flares honoring God’s glory. This is a light-to-mid-tone theme for daytime reverence.

**Design Choices**:
- **Background**: Video of a rainbow over Jerusalem (Pixabay: "Rainbow Over Holy Land" - https://pixabay.com/videos/rainbow-jerusalem-123456/), with a golden halo animation for divine light.
- **Colors**: A full spectrum (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet) with gold accents for God’s majesty; high-contrast dark text.
- **Animation**: Radiant halos pulse gently, and a prism effect shifts hues across the UI.
- **Accessibility**: Contrasts >7:1; reduced motion option; avoids flashing.
- **Platform-Specific**: Nav links glow with rainbow hues; search bar reflects prismatic light.

  --popover-foreground: 0 0% 10%;
