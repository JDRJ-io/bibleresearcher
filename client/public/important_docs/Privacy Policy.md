# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 1, 2025

Anointed.io is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information

* Email address
* Display name
* Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)

### Usage Data

* Logins and session activity
* Verse navigation and search actions
* Click and interaction events

### Content Data

* Bookmarks, notes, highlights, labels, and tags
* Forum posts and other community submissions

### Payment & Donation Data

* Name and email (as provided during donation checkout)
* Payment method details (processed **securely by Stripe**)
* Transaction history (amount, date, and status)

We do **not** collect or store full credit card numbers, bank information, or government-issued IDs.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* Provide and maintain the Anointed.io platform
* Sync your study data across devices
* Personalize your user experience
* Send important account or platform notices
* Process donations securely through Stripe
* Detect, prevent, and address technical or security issues

---

## 3. How We Store and Protect Data

* All user data is stored in **Supabase (PostgreSQL + object storage)** in the United States.
* All data is **encrypted in transit (TLS)** and **at rest**.
* **Row-Level Security (RLS)** ensures users can only access their own account data.
* We do **not** sell, rent, or trade personal information.

---

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We share minimal data with trusted third-party processors essential to operate the platform:

| Provider     | Purpose                                                                           | Status                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **Stripe**   | Donation processing and secure payment handling                                   | **Active**                   |
| **Postmark** | Transactional email delivery (sign-ups, confirmations, receipts, password resets) | **Active**                   |
| **Sentry**   | Crash/error reporting (anonymized IDs only)                                       | Planned for post-beta launch |

Stripe and Postmark only receive the minimum necessary data to perform their services.
We do **not** share your information with advertisers or data brokers.
